2048 puzzle is a funny, addictive and a very simple number puzzle game. Just join the numbers and get to the 2048 tile! Be ready for a new challenge! 

HOW TO PLAY: 
Swipe (Up, Down, Left, Right) to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one and the number is doubled. When 2048 tile is created, the player wins! 8 .. 16 ... 1024 .. 2048.
